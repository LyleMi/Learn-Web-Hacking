## Consistency Rules

- Use title case for section and navigation titles.
- When retaining Chinese title counterparts, use `English Title (中文标题)`.
- Prefer established security terminology over literal word-by-word translation.
- Keep technical acronyms such as SQL, XSS, CSRF, SSRF, XXE, JWT, OAuth, SAML, RASP, and WAF uppercase.
- Preserve code, commands, URLs, payloads, and protocol names unless the surrounding prose requires explanation.
- If a term is uncertain, keep the Chinese counterpart for traceability and update this glossary when the decision is settled.
